Why Clean, Functional Gutters Matter

Your gutters are one of the most important protective systems on your home. When gutters are clean, functional, and properly maintained, they channel water safely away from your foundation, walls, and landscaping. Clogged or damaged gutters allow water to back up and overflow, creating serious problems.

Without proper gutter function, water damage can lead to foundation cracks, basement flooding, rotted fascia and soffits, damaged landscaping, and interior mold growth—all expensive repairs. Regular gutter cleaning and maintenance prevent these costly issues before they start.

Gutter Questions Answered

How often should gutters be cleaned?

▼

We recommend gutter cleaning at least twice per year—once in fall after leaves drop, and again in spring. If you have many trees nearby, more frequent cleaning may be necessary. Regular cleaning prevents clogs and water damage.

What happens if gutters aren't maintained?

▼

Clogged gutters overflow, allowing water to run down your foundation, causing basement flooding, foundation cracks, and landscaping damage. Water can also damage fascia, soffits, and create mold growth in walls and attics.

Do you install gutter guards or leaf protection?

▼

Yes! We offer several gutter protection systems that reduce debris and minimize the need for frequent cleaning. These systems allow water to flow freely while blocking leaves and debris from entering gutters.

Can you repair my existing gutters?

▼

Absolutely! We repair sagging gutters, leaking seams, broken hangers, and damaged sections. If repairs aren't cost-effective, we recommend gutter replacement with quality materials and proper installation.

How long do new gutters last?

▼

Quality gutters properly installed typically last 20-30 years depending on climate and maintenance. We use premium materials and ensure proper slope and drainage so your gutters protect your home for decades.
