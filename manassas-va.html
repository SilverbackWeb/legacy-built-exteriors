Common Questions from Manassas Homeowners

We had a hail storm recently. How do I know if my roof was damaged?

Hail damage to asphalt shingles often isn't visible from the ground. Signs include small circular bruises or impact marks on shingles, dented metal components like vents and flashing, and granule accumulation in gutters. We provide free post-storm inspections and will photograph any damage we find so you have documentation if you need to file an insurance claim.

Do I need a permit for roofing work in Manassas?

It depends on whether your property is within Manassas city limits or in the unincorporated county. Both the City of Manassas and Prince William County require building permits for full roof replacements. We determine the correct permitting jurisdiction for your address and handle the entire process on your behalf.

My house is in a Manassas neighborhood from the 1980s. What are the most common exterior issues?

Homes of that era in Manassas frequently present with aging three-tab or early architectural shingles, original aluminum or vinyl gutters that have detached or corroded, wood soffit and fascia components that have rotted, and vinyl siding that has cracked, faded, or is no longer properly sealed at penetrations. A free inspection will identify exactly what's present and what actually needs attention.
