Siding Questions Answered

What is James Hardie siding and why is it better?

▼

James Hardie fiber cement siding is a premium material made from wood fiber, cement, and sand. It's more durable than vinyl, won't rot or warp, resists fire and pests, and maintains its beauty for 25+ years with minimal maintenance. It's the industry's gold standard.

How long does a full siding replacement take?

▼

Most siding replacements take 3-7 days depending on home size and siding type. We work efficiently to minimize disruption, and all work is completed with proper weather protection.

What siding colors and styles do you offer?

▼

We offer a wide range of colors, profiles, and finishes in both James Hardie and premium vinyl options. Our team will help you select the perfect style to complement your home's architecture and boost curb appeal.

Do you handle insurance claims for siding damage?

▼

Yes! We work directly with insurance companies on damage claims. We'll document damage, help with the claim process, and coordinate repairs to maximize your coverage.

Will new siding save money on energy bills?

▼

Modern siding with proper insulation can improve energy efficiency and reduce heating/cooling costs. Plus, beautiful new siding increases home value, often returning the investment when you sell.
